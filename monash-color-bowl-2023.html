TRESHAN

Monash Color-Bowl 2023

An autonomous robot that can transport pucks to a particular region of the competition arena based on the colour of the puck and then use these pucks to knock down bowling-pins through a flicking action. This Robot was built from scratch by design on SOLIDWORKS to 3D printing, Assembly and Automation using C language on PSoC CY8C5888LTI-LP097.
