Heart Rate Monitor using PSoC

Developed a precise heart rate monitor utilizing the advanced capabilities of PSOC CY8C5888LTI-LP097 microcontroller. Integrated with analog sensors, the system ensures accurate readings in real-time. This project showcases the synergy of digital and analog electronics, offering a reliable solution for monitoring vital signs.
